Play Puzzle Games

Welcome to Puzzle Arcadia! Below, you'll find detailed instructions on how to play our puzzle games.

All of our games are keyboard and button compatible, allowing you to enjoy a seamless gaming experience. Simply use the arrow keys on your keyboard or click the directional buttons provided on the screen to navigate through the games.

To start a new game, you can press the "Enter" key on your keyboard or click the "New Game" button.

Hangman

Hangman is a word-guessing game where you must guess the hidden word before the hangman is fully drawn.

How to Play:

  1. Click on a letter from the alphabet provided to guess a letter.
  2. If the letter is correct, it will fill in the blank spaces in the word.
  3. If the letter is incorrect, a part of the hangman will be drawn.
  4. Continue guessing letters until you either guess the word correctly or the hangman is fully drawn.
  5. If you guess the word correctly, you win! If the hangman is fully drawn, you lose.

2048

2048 is a sliding block puzzle game where you combine numbered tiles to reach the 2048 tile.

How to Play:

  1. Use the arrow keys or directional buttons to slide the tiles in the desired direction.
  2. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  3. Continue sliding and merging tiles to reach the 2048 tile.
  4. If you fill the board and cannot make any more moves, the game is over.

Sudoku

Sudoku is a number-placement puzzle where you must fill a 9x9 grid with digits so that each column, row, and 3x3 section contains all of the digits from 1 to 9.

How to Play:

  1. Enter a number by clicking on the empty cell and inputting the desired digit.
  2. Ensure that each row, column, and 3x3 section contains all of the digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.
  3. Continue filling in the grid until you've successfully completed the puzzle!

Main symbols

  1. make an hypothesis
  2. validate hypothesis
  3. rule out hypothesis
  4. restart same grid
  5. generate a new grid
